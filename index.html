The Louisville Rowing Club offers its members rowing equipment and volunteer coaching, storage for private rowing boats, introductory rowing for novices, regatta entry and social gatherings. All activities and operation of the Louisville Rowing Club are promoted and conducted by volunteers. There is no paid staff.
